Standards & Compliance

We Operate to the Highest Standards

FC Fleet Limited is committed to full compliance across all areas of our operations — from driver checks to vehicle roadworthiness and data handling.

📋

Driver Licence & DBS Checks

All drivers undergo full DVLA licence verification and DBS (Disclosure and Barring Service) checks before joining our team. We maintain up-to-date records for every driver in our fleet.

Mandatory for All Drivers
🔧

Vehicle Safety Inspections

Every vehicle in our fleet undergoes regular pre-use safety inspections in line with DVSA guidelines. Walkaround checks, tyre conditions, brakes, lights, and load security are verified before every deployment.

DVSA Compliant
🛡️

Fleet Insurance & Liability

All FC Fleet vehicles operate with full commercial fleet insurance, covering hire and reward use, goods in transit, and public liability — giving our clients and partners full peace of mind.

Fully Insured
⚖️

Working Time & Hours Compliance

We strictly adhere to the Working Time Regulations and EU/UK drivers' hours rules. Driver hours are monitored and managed to ensure legal compliance and road safety at all times.

Working Time Regulations
📊

KRI Monitoring

We actively monitor Key Risk Indicators (KRIs) across our fleet operations — including near-miss incidents, vehicle defect rates, driver behaviour, and delivery exceptions — to identify and mitigate risk proactively.

Risk Managed
🌿

Environmental Responsibility

FC Fleet is committed to reducing our carbon footprint through route optimisation, regular emission checks, and an ongoing commitment to transitioning to cleaner vehicle options as part of our long-term fleet strategy.

Eco Conscious
📁

Data Protection & GDPR

All client data, driver records, and operational information are handled in full compliance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

UK GDPR Compliant
🏅

Service Level Agreements (SLAs)

We operate under clearly defined SLAs with all partners, including Yodel and InPost. Our compliance with agreed delivery windows, vehicle standards, and reporting obligations is tracked and maintained rigorously.

SLA Compliant
